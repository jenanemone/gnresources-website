Our Story

Welcome to GNR, a story of entrepreneurship and perseverance. Our company was founded by Mark Ganley in 2018 with a simple mission: to help a friend who had a small gold mining claim in Alaska. Little did we know this humble beginning would be the start of something much bigger. After getting the company up and running, our friend decided to pursue other ventures, leaving us with GNR, but we didn't give up.

In late 2019, an opportunity arose through some consulting work with a company in Oklahoma. We committed GNR to building a lab and leasing it to Briton Green Resources. They had expertise in medical processing, but lacked the capital and the wherwithal to navigate the regulations. GNR was the perfect partner. We have completed the build-out of the lab and helped Briton obtain approvals to operate.

GNR has since diversified by renting out temporary housing to the Cherokee Indian Nation, who are doing a 3-year construction project and have engaged us to provide trailers. This gives us another customer base to serve. At GNR, we believe in finding good business opportunities that are synergistic to our business model. Thank you for joining us on this journey. We invite you to learn more about GNR and our commitment to excellence in everything we do. What can GNR do for YOU???
